Why Fuzzy ?

Fuzzy numbers are useful to analyze real expressions and unify them, for example using geometric areas for represent this ones and then compare the "areas" of every decision to get the highest or the largest area of union.

Business Intelligence

In BI the most complex and worrisome task is to use all this mass of data to develop algorithms that allow well-founded Making Decisions and achievable with the technology we have. Basically, we are interested in forming business groups to take responsibility to indicate possible ways to achieve business goals, with multiple objectives that meet not only the members of the group, but showing results of the experience of specialists. All this serves as the basis to be taken as a general objective of this work to the development of efficient and effective for group decision making algorithms.

done

Objetives

Specific objective 1 - Developing a favorable environment for the process leading to obtain a theoretical process model Decision Making, where as main elements current trends are integrated into the development of software systems, characteristics of operating systems in force, use of multivalent logic process and aspects taken from process optimization.
Specific objective 2 - Selecting a programming language that allows placing developed models and data for use in the cloud. The purpose is clear: to develop models accessible to users anywhere.
Specific objective 3 - To validate the developed models using real problems, in all its complexity, to achieve a suitable measuring parameter to current business needs.

A quick way to rate your possible problem solutions based on criterias defined by your decision group

How it Works

This method get the quantity of decisors on your group, the ranking of those decisors, the criteria of selection, the valuation of every decisor upon this one, and then the final valuation of the alternatives, all this selections are represented by triangular fuzzy numbers to be analyzed and get the best choice for the group , and a properly rating of the alternatives; Check out the following video:
